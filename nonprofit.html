3. Understanding UX

I understand User Experience to be the process of designing products that are useful, easy to use, and pleasant to interact with.It's about enhancing the experience that people have while interacting with a product, and making sure they find usefulness in what is provided.

Project Concept & Goal

The concept and goal of this website is to offer straight to the point information on ways to donate to a non profit organization by making the site easy to navigate by offering strait to the point buttons to get the job accomplished.

5 Pinpoints I wanted to make sure I address

  • Navigation Tabs
  • Search Area
  • Consistency & Structure
  • Clarity
  • Keep secondary actions secondary
